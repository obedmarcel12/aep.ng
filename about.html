Who we are

With years of experience in the Oil and Gas industry, Advanced
energy partners have evolved into a leading organisation in the
sector. AEP works with clients to achieve their short term goals
keeping a focus on the long term plan delivering premium
solutions ensuring that our clients operational satisfaction is not
just met but exceeded.

Certified Service
Provider

AEP has an outstanding track record of various multinational
projects completed on time and on budget. Our dedication and
commitment to excellence is obvious when considering the very
high standards of all completed projects.

To be the premium Oil and Gas engineering firm of choice dedicated to

in the industry.

Our Mission

What we do

Advanced Energy Partners is an indigenous engineering services provider
with years of experience in the industry. Our operations cover a vast scope
including oil & gas, electricity &
power, engineering procurement & construction, manpower & training and energy development.
